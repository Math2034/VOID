## The Concept
**"EMBRACE THE VOID"** channels underground rave culture through gothic poster aesthetics. This isn't a website—it's a **digital flyer** designed to create urgency, capture attention, and convert visitors into ticket buyers in seconds.

## Project Objective
Build a **conversion-first landing page** that:
- Sells out events through visual impact and psychological urgency
- Eliminates distractions with single-purpose design
- Converts 80%+ of traffic on mobile devices
- Loads instantly for impulse purchases
